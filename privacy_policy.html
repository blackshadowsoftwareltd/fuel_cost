Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Black Shadow Software Ltd ("we", "our", "us") built the Fuel Cost app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Fuel Cost is designed with privacy in mind. All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers.

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Fuel entries (liters, price, cost, date, odometer reading)
  • Vehicle information (name, fuel type, tank capacity)
  • Maintenance records (type, cost, date, mileage)
  • App preferences (theme, currency, monthly budget)

Google Drive Backup (Optional)

If you choose to connect your Google account, the app can backup your fuel entries to your personal Google Drive. This feature is entirely optional.

  • We use Google Sign-In to authenticate your Google account.
  • We request access only to the app-specific folder on your Google Drive (driveAppdataScope). We cannot access any other files on your Drive.
  • The backup contains your fuel entry data in CSV format.
  • Your Google email address is displayed in the app solely to show which account is connected.
  • We do not store your Google credentials. Authentication is handled entirely by Google's OAuth 2.0 system.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties. Your data stays on your device and, if you opt in, on your personal Google Drive.

Analytics and Tracking

Fuel Cost does not include any analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or tracking tools. We do not collect usage statistics or behavioral data.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally using your device's secure storage mechanisms.
  • Google Drive backups are protected by your Google account security.
  • We recommend keeping your device locked with a PIN, password, or biometric authentication.

Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete individual fuel entries or maintenance records from within the app.
  • Clear all data from Settings > Data Management.
  • Disconnect Google Drive and delete backups from Settings > Google Drive Backup.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Fuel Cost is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
