Privacy Policy — Fuel Cost

Effective date: 19 April 2026
Publisher: Black Shadow Software Ltd
Contact: blackshadowsoftwareltd@gmail.com

This is the privacy policy for Fuel Cost, a mobile application that tracks your fuel spend, mileage, and vehicle maintenance. All your records live on your device, with an optional backup to your own Google Drive.

Plain English, no lawyer-speak. If anything here is unclear or you'd like your data removed, email us at the address above.

1. What data we collect

We don't run a server. We don't collect anything from you. The data below lives only on your device, and only when you enter it yourself:

  • Fuel entries (liters, price, cost, date, odometer reading)
  • Vehicle information (name, fuel type, tank capacity)
  • Maintenance records (type, cost, date, mileage)
  • App preferences (theme, currency, monthly budget)

What we do not collect

  • No user accounts. No login. No email. No phone number.
  • No analytics. No tracking. No usage telemetry.
  • No advertising SDKs. No ad IDs. No fingerprinting.
  • No crash reporting services.
  • No contact list, calendar, location, microphone, or any sensor.

2. Data stored on your device

Everything below stays on your phone. It is not shared with us or any third party.

3. Google Drive Backup (optional)

If you choose to connect your Google account, Fuel Cost can back up your fuel entries to your own Google Drive. This feature is entirely optional and off by default.

  • We use Google Sign-In to authenticate your Google account.
  • We request access only to the app-specific folder on your Google Drive (driveAppdataScope). We cannot access any other files on your Drive.
  • The backup contains your fuel-entry data in CSV format.
  • Your Google email address is displayed in the app solely to show which account is connected.
  • We do not store your Google credentials. Authentication is handled entirely by Google's OAuth 2.0 system.

Google's handling of data stored on your Drive is governed by their own policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Terms of Service

4. Third parties

Aside from the optional Google Drive backup above, there are no third-party services — no analytics providers, ad networks, A/B-testing services, crash reporters, or tracking SDKs of any kind embedded in Fuel Cost.

We do not request camera, microphone, location, contacts, calendar, SMS, phone, body sensors, or any background services.

6. Data deletion

You are always in full control of your data:

  • Delete individual fuel entries or maintenance records from within the app.
  • Clear all data from Settings > Data Management.
  • Disconnect Google Drive and delete backups from Settings > Google Drive Backup.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

7. Children

Fuel Cost is not directed at children under 13 (or under the relevant age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Security

Local data lives in a sandboxed application directory on your device managed by the operating system. Google Drive backups, if enabled, are protected by your Google account security (we recommend enabling two-factor authentication on that account). We recommend keeping your device locked with a PIN, password, or biometric authentication.

9. Your rights

  • Access — everything is on your device, visible inside the app.
  • Delete — use in-app delete buttons, clear all data in Settings, or uninstall Fuel Cost.
  • Object to processing — stop using the app at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority if you believe we've mishandled your data.

10. Changes to this policy

If we materially change how the app handles data, we'll update this document and bump the effective date at the top.
